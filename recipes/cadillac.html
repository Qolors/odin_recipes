Cadillac Margarita

Ooo so tasty!

A gentleman's classic./h2>

This margarita is as classy as it gets.

Things you need

  • 1 ounce of Grand Marnier
  • 1 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 ounce of tequila
  • 1 ounce of Countreau
  • ice per request

What to do with the things

  1. Rim the glass with salt.
  2. Add ice and shake shake shake!
  3. Strain into a margarita glass and serve with a lim wedge.Finished!
