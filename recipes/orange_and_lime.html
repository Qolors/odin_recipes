Orange and Lime Margarita

Ooo so tasty!

A Sunkissed Surprise

This margarita, made with freshly squeezed orange juice, lime juice, and agave for a natural sweetener, is light, refreshing, and not too sweet. Salt or sugar rim optional.

Things you need

  • 2 ounces of freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 ounce of tequila
  • 1 tablespoon of agave syrup
  • ice per request
  • orange twist

What to do with the things

  1. Pour orange juice, lime juice, tequila, and agave syrup into a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add ice and shake shake shake!
  3. Strain into a margarita glass and serve with an orange twist. Finished!
