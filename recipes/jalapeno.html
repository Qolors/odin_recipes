Jalapeno Margarita

Ooo so tasty!

A spicy take on Margaritas

This margarita, garnished with jalapeno slices, is a spice lover's delight!

Things you need

  • 1 ounce of cointreau
  • 1 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 ounce of tequila
  • ice per request
  • jalapeno pepper slices

What to do with the things

  1. Rim the glass with salt.
  2. Add ice and shake shake shake!
  3. Strain into a margarita glass and garnish with jalapeno slices. Finished!
